1. BACKLOG FRAMEWORK AND STORY FORMAT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
The Sovereign Forge product backlog is organized using the standard
Agile Epic > Story hierarchy. Stories are the primary unit of work
tracked in sprint planning. Epics are delivered across one or more
sprints and represent complete functional capabilities.

STORY FORMAT:
  Story ID:          SOVEREIGN-XXX
  Epic:              EPIC-00N
  Title:             Short description (verb + noun)
  Description:       What the system does and why it matters
  Acceptance Criteria: Minimum 3 testable criteria (numbered)
  Story Points:      Fibonacci: 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21
  Status:            DONE / IN PROGRESS / PLANNED
  Notes:             Additional context, blockers, dependencies

STORY ID NUMBERING:
  SOVEREIGN-001 through SOVEREIGN-005:  EPIC-001 stories
  SOVEREIGN-006 through SOVEREIGN-010:  EPIC-002 stories
  SOVEREIGN-011 through SOVEREIGN-015:  EPIC-003 stories
  SOVEREIGN-016 through SOVEREIGN-020:  EPIC-004 stories
  SOVEREIGN-021 through SOVEREIGN-025+: EPIC-005 stories

STATUS DEFINITIONS:
  DONE:         Story is complete. All acceptance criteria verified.
                Code is merged to main. CI passes.
  IN PROGRESS:  Story is actively being worked on in current sprint.
  PLANNED:      Story is in backlog; not yet started.
  BLOCKED:      Story cannot progress without resolution of a blocker.
